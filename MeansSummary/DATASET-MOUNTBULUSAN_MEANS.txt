The Philippine Institute of Volcanology and Seismology Phivolcs has allayed fears that a major eruption in Mount Bulusan is imminent following the steam-driven explosion that happened on Friday morning.
Mount Bulusan in Sorsogon province has spewed ashes on Friday afternoon drifting North West to the town of Juban, the Philippine Institute of Volcanology and Seismology PHIVOLCS) said.
“It is a common volcanic activity in Mount Bulusan, ” Phivolcs director Renato Solidum said, referring to the five-minute phreatic eruption that sent ash plume as high as two kilometers.
