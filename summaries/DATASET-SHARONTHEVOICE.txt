Veteran actress-singer Sharon Cuneta is excited to be one of the coaches on the third season of The Voice Kids.
This, she said, is the reason why she has not been active on social media for quite a while.
Sharon confirmed the news in an interview with ABS-CBN, Monday, May 2. One of the first surprises for you is [that I will be part of The Voice Kids with – of course – Bamboo and Ms. Lea Salonga, and I ca n't wait to be with them and to be with the kids and to be with you every week, she said.
