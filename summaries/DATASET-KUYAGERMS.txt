He lived a full life, touched so many hearts through the years, and helped make dreams come true for most of the biggest stars in the Philippine entertainment industry, he said.
He also initiated the Walk of Fame in Eastwood, Quezon City, which is the local counterpart of the Walk of Fame in Hollywood, USA to recognize artists, broadcasters, singers and others who made significant contributions to the showbiz industry.
The Master Showman will be greatly missed by his friends and fans both here and abroad, especially those in show business, where he devoted the best years of his life.
